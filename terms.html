Services of WTI

Often people are unable to file their RTI queries for want of time or proper means. We make it much easier for you. All you have to do is fill our application form. It is much more convenient, hassle-free and efficient. You can pay your application fee online(through Debit card/Credit card/online banking) or by challan.

What WTI promises.

We as a team, take action on any and every RTI application as and when we receive it without compromising with its content. We truly believe in timely delivery of our services. Our team functions on a strong base to solely work towards transparency. Our services are affordable and quick.
