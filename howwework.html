RTI FILING PROCESS



Here is the whole Process


1. Apply RTI Online

Go to apply page and file RTI application online. Fill personal details and write in simple language what information is required.

2. We Draft Your RTI

Our WTI Expert team will draft your RTI application as per best practices, find the correct government office and send the drafted RTI application for your approval.

3. We File Your RTI

Once RTI application gets approved, our logistics team will take care of paying the required government fees and send RTI application via registered post.

4. You Get Information

You will get information from the government within 30 days of filing as mandated by the RTI Act.


What we will do for you?

  • Govt. Fees will be paid by IPO, DD, Court Fee Stamp as necessary.
  • RTI Application will be filed by Registered or Speed Post.
  • Attachments will be sent along with the application.
